Allison Brekosky

student. singer. musician.



Early Life Allison Elizabeth Brekosky was born October 23rd, 1998 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Always living within Pittsburgh's tri-state area has been a blessing for the singer, as the city atmosphere has attracted some big names, allowing Allison to work with professionals from all over the country. She grew up with both parents, Dave and Jill Brekosky, and one older brother, Jacob Brekosky. Her biggest musical inspiration were both of her grandmothers, her paternal grandmother, Florence, gifting her her first piano at a very young age, and her maternal grandmother being the singer of the family.
